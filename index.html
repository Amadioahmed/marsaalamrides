    FAQs

    How can the driver know if the flight is delayed?

    Marsa Alam Taxi Services monitors flight arrivals live at Marsa Alam Airport.

    Are there any extra charge if the flight is delayed?

    There’s no extra charge if your flight is late.

    How can the driver recognize me at Marsa Alam Airport?

    Your driver will be promptly waiting for you outside the airport, anytime your flight arrives. He holds up a sign with your name for easy identification.

    What can I do if we missed our taxi driver at Marsa Alam airport?

    In the unlikely event you miss each other outside the airport, please contact Marsa Alam Taxi Services directly at mobile numbers +201111101563. Tell us where you are and what you look like and your driver will reach out to you.

    How can I pay? do I need to pay in advance ?

    You will pay cash to the driver after you arrive at your destination to your hotel. We accept euro, Egyptian pound or dollar. Please give him your room number to advise him for your return trip. He will be waiting for you on time at the hotel reception or resort or any other destination you wish to be picked up.

    What time should I show up before the flight take of time at Marsa Alam Airport?

    Please be alerted to show up at the airport at least two hours before your departure.
